Gate Info & Quantum Concepts

  • H (Hadamard): Puts qubit in superposition
  • X (Pauli-X): Bit-flip
  • Z (Pauli-Z): Phase-flip
  • Y (Pauli-Y): Combines X & Z
  • S, T: Phase rotation gates
  • CNOT: Entangles 2 qubits
  • Superposition: Qubit exists in 0 and 1 simultaneously
  • Entanglement: Qubits correlated even far apart
