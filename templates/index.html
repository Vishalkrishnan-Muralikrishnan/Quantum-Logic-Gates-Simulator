Gate Info & Quantum Concepts

  • H (Hadamard): Puts qubit in superposition |0>+|1>
  • X (Pauli-X): Bit-flip gate (|0>↔|1>)
  • Z (Pauli-Z): Phase flip |1>→-|1>
  • CNOT: Entangles two qubits; flips target if control=1
  • Superposition: Qubit exists in both 0 and 1 states simultaneously
  • Entanglement: Qubits are correlated even when separated
